About Us

Daddy’s kitchen was established at the year 2013 from Butwal with the motto of serving people with best food and generating employment for young people within the Nation with slogan of “कायापलट ल्याउन्छौ स्वदेशमै,युवा उध्यमिसलताको सम्मान गरौ|” It is also one of the first “Food-Truck Chain” in Nepal. We are almost successful in satisfying consumers with our surpassing food. We have got outlets in different cities with best locations. We have got different forms of outlets such as:

  • Food-Truck
  • Food-Trailer
  • Food-Court
  • Cafe and Restaurant.
